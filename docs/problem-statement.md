At IIT, student attendance is currently recorded manually using attendance sheets. This method can cause errors, missing records, inconsistencies, and data loss. It also creates extra workload for lecturers, academic coordinators, and administrative staff when recording, checking, and preparing attendance reports.

Students also have limited visibility of their attendance records. Because of this, they may only become aware of poor attendance after receiving formal warnings, which can be too late to take corrective action.

The proposed Smart Student Attendance Recording System aims to solve these issues by providing a centralised digital platform for attendance management. The system will allow students to mark attendance using QR-based attendance, view their attendance records, submit absence justifications, and receive notifications. Lecturers and administrative staff will be able to monitor attendance, generate reports, and identify students with low attendance earlier.

The goal of this project is to improve attendance accuracy, reduce manual workload, increase student awareness, and support better academic decision-making.
